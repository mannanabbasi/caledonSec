Welcome to Caledon Security

Caledon Security provides a customer focused professional security service in and around London and the home counties. Our proactive approach towards security ensures that you receive a bespoke personal service which is specifically customized to meet every aspect of your corporate security requirements

At Caledon Security, we understand that the need to secure your business, home, property and family is becoming increasingly paramount. Therefore, Caledon Security prides itself in maintaining the best codes of practice by offering first class, fully SIA licensed, security professionals for a broad range of commercial and residential clients to deliver the ultimate peace of mind every time. We certainly live up to our name in terms of our service.

As a distinguished service provider, we believe in listening to our Clients needs and turning them into a core basis for our effective service provision. Each of our contracts are unique and we ensure that we work in extremely close partnership with each of our client management teams in order to render our best possible service.

We tailor a solution to your needs and budget. Our goal is protection and safety of your people, buildings, assets and reputation.
