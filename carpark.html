Car Park Security

Car Park Security is not very exhilarating, but it is essential when we talk about car parking in general. Car parks need to be safe and secure, for both people and vehicles. That is why Caledon Security are the best when it comes to car parking security. As all of our staff are SIA licensed and have received training to NVQ standards we have reduced levels of car crime in our car parks. We will dramatically continue to improve car park security for our clients. We can implement, integrated internal and external security systems and security guarding. We can provide the most innovate car park services in London and other major cities in the UK. We understand how important it is for your customers to feel comfortable leaving their vehicles on your premises, which is why we can offer much more than just putting a security guard on site. Our car park security can extend to maintaining the entire facility, which can include mobile patrols, key handling, CCTV monitoring and much more.

Caledon Security can offer any one of our services around the clock 365 days a year.
