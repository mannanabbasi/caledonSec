Subcontractor

If you are looking to form a subcontractor relationship with a security company in and around London that guarantees the highest quality security guards then you need to look no further.

Our subcontract security guard services are an excellent solution for security companies that lack the manpower to meet the requirements of a larger job or are short staffed due to holidays or sickness. If you are missing out on jobs because you do not have the capacity, then working with us will provide the perfect solution.

We can provide both short and long term subcontract security guards for any type of project or on-going requirement. Whether it's for one day or night or a period of several months, you will find our service reliable, professional and exceptionally flexible.

Each of our security guards are fully vetted and we guarantee that working with us, you will experience the very best in professionalism, expertise and experience so that your reputation is maintained and enhanced. All of our security guards are SIA licensed, CRB checked, conversant with health and safety regulations and fully trained to very high standards.

Our security company can supply guards in plain uniform. Alternatively, you can supply uniform for them in order to maintain your own image. We regularly subcontract door supervisors, security guards and other security or event management personnel to a range of security companies throughout London and the home counties and we are confident that you will be as equally satisfied with our services. Subcontracting security services to us gives you the flexibility of not having to advertise, recruit or employ staff temporarily. Furthermore, your client's site will be under our security insurance cover.

For a no obligation estimate on your specific requirements, please contact us at your convenience.
