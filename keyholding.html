Key Holding Services

All our staff are fully vetted, CRB checked and SIA licensed, including the additional training requirements for key holding and response services.

Whether you require short term cover in the event of n alarm activation, or mobile key holding services to allow contractor access or assist with tenant lock outs we have the solution. Caledon security can arrange short-term, emergency manned guarding for a upon the instructions of a client and at short notice.

We provide a comprehensive range of secure key holding facilities for both commercial and residential properties. Our mobile team are on duty 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. We keep your property safe at all times and you can trust us to respond to alarm activations.

We will keep hold of your keys with your permission and will enter the property as and when we are alerted to possible intruders by the monitoring station. Our key holders will be first on site to deal with any situation and will remain on your property until it is completely secure. Once on site, the officer will perform external and internal checks and contact our centre immediately if emergency services are required. In the case of serious incident we will liaise with police officers and obtain full details on insurance claims or any other reporting matter.

Why choose our key holding service?

  • Rapid response to commercial and residential alarm activations.

  • Flexibility and ability to deal with emergencies.

  • 24 hour control room and back up.

  • Mobile patrols.

  • Vacant property checks.

  • Temporary static guarding.
