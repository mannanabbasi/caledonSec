Static Security Guarding Services

Our Static security Officers are far from stationary, the vital part of their duty is to conduct regular and thorough security patrols.

The main reasons for security patrols are to:

  • Prevent Accidents

  • Detect and prevent offences

  • Protect against Health and Safety risks

  • Prevent waste of your company's resources

  • Prevent ﬁre, ﬂood and damae to the property

These security patrols provide an added level of vigilance and intelligence gathering and often prevent an incident from occurring in the ﬁrst place.

These security patrols vary in time and route, in order to ensure the element of surprise, should an incident occur. Should n incident occur, it is logged in the appropriate incident book which becomes important evidence, if and when required.

As environmental issues are becoming more important due to the cost of wasted energy, our security operatives can check for and switch off unnecessary lighting and equipment saving you money by reducing energy bills. This will also help to reduce your company's carbon footprint.

Static Security Guards ensure your staff are obeying the rules and regulations as laid down by your company. This can be anything from enforcing the no smoking policy to safeguarding the physical and intellectual property of your organization.

All of our Static Guarding operatives report hourly to the Control Centre. If contact is lost between any of our guards, an immediate investigation is begun to ascertain the cause.

The advantage of a Static Security Officer over CCTV or alarm monitoring is to get a much quicker response to problems and issues, than is possible through other alarm response solutions. Most criminals would anticipate that their crime needs to be committed within five minutes of gaining access, so other response times are too slow in comparison with a Static Guard who is based on site.

One of the popular misconceptions for the public is that our Security Officers patrol to catch intruders. In reality, due to the security measures we have already put in place,intrusions are rare.
