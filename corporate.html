Corporate Security Services

Caledon Security provides a fully managed reception and corporate security services

Our corporate and reception services provide you with highly professional staff on a full-time basis, or to seamlessly supplement your staff for holiday, sickness cover or heavy workloads.

We ensure that our clients have a team that is an extension of the brand image of their organization. Our team work closely with your staff to provide first class service for clients, residents and visitors alike.

Our staff provide meet and greet services, switchboard call handling, maintain front of house security, monitor movements and ensure that only authorized personnel gain access to restricted areas, in a friendly but authoritative and professional manner.

Professionally presented, wearing either your or our own uniform your receptionist or concierge staff from Caledon Security will be fully briefed on your business and their role as professional within. They will also conduct themselves appropriately and respectably as a member of your team, in order to meet and exceed the expectations required for highly reputable representation of your organization.
