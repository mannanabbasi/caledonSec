About us

At Caledon Security Services we pride ourselves in our ability to deliver the highest levels of service possible, from the initial risk assessment onward you can be rest assured you are in safe hands. To ensure the best fit, we ensure all our personnel are trained to the highest standards and stringently vetted both professionally and personally. Our security personnel are provided with the most comprehensive security training before they are deployed. Our thoroughly vetted, fully trained and experienced security officers maintain best codes of practice. Our security personnel undergo regular refresher training to keep up high quality and standards of the organization. The trainers are also outsourced from the best in the industry, to ensure our professionals are trained for specific task in hand.

Our customer focused approach ensures we remain hands-on and able to address your on-going requirements as and when they arise. In addition, the commitment of all our employees, across the entire team means you will always receive an outstanding service.

Our operatives are discreet, highly trained and above all are dedicated to the welfare of the client. Available as individuals, our personnel are skilled in all areas of high risk scenarios. We recognize that client servicing and employee welfare are two key areas. Our employee and client retention rates provide the perfect proof that we maintain the best codes of practice. We know how important it is to listen and understand our clients' needs so that we are able to deliver the best service and solutions.

Our Control Centre is manned around the clock, 365 days of the year and utilizes the very latest security technology. Our Security Project Management capability can design and deliver optimized security solutions encompassing manned security, mobile response, CCTV, access control systems and other related services. Caledon Security can arrange a security review for your site and recommend the most appropriate security coverage to meet the requirements of your business.

We can provide smart and trained security personnel to cope with the specialized requirements of hospitals, hotels, restaurants, clubs, resorts, etc. These services are acquired by corporate, government agencies, private companies, hotels, residential units, and many others. We have professionals with expertise in hotel management and handling of high level security. Our state-of-the-art training and development facility consists of full-time professionals on permanent roles to train our specialists. Our guards services division provides permanent dedicated armed/unarmed security guards capable of performing duties such as patrol access control reception, console operation, communication and crowd control, with a history of reliable and clean background.

We evaluate the total requirement of our client's property and design security programs to ensure a high level of security.
